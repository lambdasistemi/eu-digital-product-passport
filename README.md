# EU Digital Product Passport

Research, schemas, and examples for the EU Digital Product Passport (DPP) under the Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR).

## What is it

The Digital Product Passport is a structured data record attached to physical products via a data carrier (QR code, RFID, NFC). It provides standardized information about a product's sustainability, circularity, material composition, and supply chain — accessible to consumers, authorities, and economic operators.
