# DPP Rollout Timeline

## Already in force

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 17 Aug 2023 | Battery Regulation (EU) 2023/1542 enters into force |
| 18 Jul 2024 | ESPR (EU) 2024/1781 enters into force |
| 18 Feb 2025 | Battery carbon footprint declaration required |
| 18 Aug 2025 | Battery carbon footprint performance classes |

## Upcoming milestones

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 18 Feb 2027 | **Battery passport mandatory** — EV and industrial batteries > 2 kWh |
| 2026-2027 | First ESPR delegated acts expected (iron/steel, textiles) |
| 2028-2030 | DPP requirements phased in for priority product groups |
| 2030+ | Broader product coverage as additional delegated acts are adopted |

## Delegated acts pipeline

The European Commission adopts delegated acts on a rolling basis. Each delegated act specifies:

- Which product category is covered
- What data fields are required in the DPP
- Transition periods for compliance
- Verification and auditing requirements

### Expected priority order

1. **Iron and steel** — most advanced, expected among the first
2. **Textiles** — strong push from the EU Textile Strategy
3. **Electronics/ICT** — high value for circularity
4. **Furniture**
5. **Tyres**
6. **Construction products** — under separate Construction Products Regulation (CPR) revision, which also references DPPs

!!! note "Dates are indicative"
    Delegated acts are adopted by the Commission and subject to Parliament/Council scrutiny. Exact dates shift frequently. Check [EUR-Lex](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/) for current status.
