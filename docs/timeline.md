## How delegated acts work

The European Commission adopts delegated acts on a rolling basis under [ESPR Art. 9](references.md#espr-art9). Each delegated act specifies:

- Which product category is covered
- What data fields are required in the DPP
- Whether the DPP is at model, batch, or item level ([Art. 9(2)(d)](references.md#espr-art9-2d))
- Transition periods for compliance (typically 18-24 months)
- Verification and auditing requirements

Once published in the Official Journal, economic operators have the specified transition period to implement the DPP.

!!! note "Dates are indicative"
    Delegated acts are adopted by the Commission and subject to Parliament/Council scrutiny. Exact dates shift frequently. The [ESPR Working Plan 2025-2030](references.md#espr-working-plan) provides the official roadmap. Check [EUR-Lex](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/) for current status.

## Interacting regulations (no standalone DPP, but linked)

These regulations do not mandate their own DPP but rely on or interact with DPP data:

| Regulation | Interaction with DPP |
|-----------|---------------------|
| [**CBAM**](references.md#cbam) (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) | DPP carbon footprint data supports CBAM compliance for cement, iron/steel, aluminium, fertilizers, electricity, hydrogen |
| **CSRD** (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive) | Product-level DPP data feeds company-level sustainability reports |
| [**EUDR**](references.md#reg-eudr) (EU Deforestation Regulation) | Supply chain traceability overlaps for rubber, wood, palm oil products |
| **Right to Repair Directive** | DPP provides repair manuals, spare parts info, repairability scores. Transposition deadline: 31 Jul 2026 |
| [**Empowering Consumers Directive**](references.md#empowering-consumers) | Bans vague green claims; DPP data substantiates environmental claims |
| [**CSDDD**](references.md#csddd) (Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence) | DPP carries product-level evidence of supply chain due diligence |
| [**Waste Framework Directive**](references.md#wfd-revision) (revision) | DPP data facilitates sorting, recycling, and EPR eco-modulation |
