    ID Article Topic
    BAT-ART77 Battery Reg. Art. 77 Battery passport requirements, responsibilities, cessation. Art. 77(3) sets the 18 Feb 2027 application date.
    BAT-ART77-1 Battery Reg. Art. 77(1) Each battery gets an individual passport
    BAT-ART77-3 Battery Reg. Art. 77(3) Access rights to passport data
    BAT-ART77-4 Battery Reg. Art. 77(4) Manufacturer responsibility for accuracy
    BAT-ART77-6A Battery Reg. Art. 77(6)(a) Repurposed battery requires new passport
    BAT-ART77-6B Battery Reg. Art. 77(6)(b) Passport ceases after recycling
    BAT-ART14 Battery Reg. Art. 14 BMS must contain SoH data
    BAT-ART14-2 Battery Reg. Art. 14(2) Read-only BMS access for purchaser or third party acting on their behalf (does not mandate a specific interface such as OBD)
    BAT-ANNEX-XIII Battery Reg. Annex XIII Mandatory data fields for battery passport
    BAT-REC46 Battery Reg. Recital 46 SoH data refreshed at least daily in BMS
    BAT-REC94 Battery Reg. Recital 94 Compliance responsibility on placing-on-market operator
    ESPR-ART9 ESPR Art. 9 DPP general requirements
    ESPR-ART9-2D ESPR Art. 9(2)(d) Delegated acts specify granularity (model/batch/item)
    ESPR-ART10 ESPR Art. 10 DPP technical design, accessibility. Art. 10(1)(h) delegates access tiers to delegated acts.
    ESPR-ART13 ESPR Art. 13 EU DPP Registry by 19 July 2026
    ESPR-ART23 ESPR Art. 23 Ban on destruction of unsold goods
