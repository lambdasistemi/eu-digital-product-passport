    How delegated acts work¶

    The European Commission adopts delegated acts on a rolling basis. Each delegated act specifies:

    • Which product category is covered
    • What data fields are required in the DPP
    • Transition periods for compliance (typically 18-24 months)
    • Verification and auditing requirements

    Once published in the Official Journal, economic operators have the specified transition period to implement the DPP for their products.

    Dates are indicative

    Delegated acts are adopted by the Commission and subject to Parliament/Council scrutiny. Exact dates shift frequently. Check EUR-Lex for current status.
